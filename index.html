About Us

Vaikuntha Computers can help you build better future using the power of computing!

Just start with our free packages and scale as you grow.

We specialise in training students as well as working professionals in accounting, web designing, programming languages, hardware, networking and basics of computers. The institution is run and managed by a seasoned professionals, who leads a team of educators and trainers having relevant domain expertise.
